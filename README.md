# Sales & Business Performance Analysis

This project presents a business-focused sales performance analysis across four stores using Python and Pandas.  
The goal is to evaluate revenue generation, product demand, customer satisfaction, and shipping cost efficiency in order to support data-driven strategic decisions.

---

## üìå Project Overview

The analysis addresses the following business questions:

- Which store generates the highest revenue?
- How do sales vary by product category across stores?
- Which stores deliver better customer satisfaction?
- What products perform best and worst?
- How efficient are shipping costs across stores?

The project combines exploratory data analysis, clear visualizations, and business interpretation to deliver actionable insights.

## ‚≠ê Key Findings

- **Store 1** leads in total revenue, making it the strongest short-term revenue driver.
- **Store 3** shows the highest average customer rating, indicating superior customer experience.
- Product demand varies significantly by store, reflecting differentiated customer preferences.
- **Store 4** benefits from the lowest average shipping cost, offering logistical efficiency.
- Best- and worst-selling products highlight opportunities for pricing and promotional optimization.

---

## üéØ Strategic Recommendation

**Store 3** is identified as the most balanced and sustainable option based on the combined evaluation of:

- Strong revenue performance (close to the top performer)
- Highest customer satisfaction score
- Leading sales in high-demand product categories
- Consistent commercial performance

Although its shipping costs are not the lowest, customer satisfaction and product demand suggest a strong value proposition that supports long-term growth.
